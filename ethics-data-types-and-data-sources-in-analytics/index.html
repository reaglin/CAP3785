CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Ethics, Data Types, and Data Sources in Analytics

This module explores the ethical responsibilities of data professionals alongside the foundational concepts of data types and sources. Learners will examine moral frameworks, fairness, bias, privacy regulations, and the practical landscape of structured and unstructured data — building the judgment needed to conduct analytics with integrity.
