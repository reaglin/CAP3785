CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Data Quality Assessment and Data Cleaning Fundamentals

This module introduces the core principles of data quality assessment and systematic data cleaning. Learners will explore key quality dimensions including accuracy, completeness, and consistency, and develop practical skills for identifying and handling missing values, duplicate records, and outliers.
