CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Advanced Excel Functions and Statistical Tools

This module develops proficiency with Excel's most powerful analytical capabilities — including lookup functions, conditional aggregation, and descriptive-statistics tools. Learners will move from individual function syntax to integrated, real-world data-analysis workflows, supported by readings, a slide deck, and a knowledge-check quiz.
