CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Data Visualization Principles and Excel Charting

This module develops the skills needed to transform raw data into clear, accurate, and compelling visual stories. Learners explore foundational visualization theory—chart selection, visual hierarchy, color strategy, and avoiding misleading representations—before applying these principles hands-on inside Microsoft Excel.
