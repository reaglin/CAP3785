CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Introduction to Data Analytics and the Analytics Lifecycle

This module establishes the foundational concepts of data analytics — what it is, why it matters, and how organizations use it to turn raw data into competitive advantage. Learners will explore the four core analytics types (descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive) and walk through the CRISP-DM lifecycle framework that structures real-world analytics projects. Use the resources below to read, review, and assess your understanding.
