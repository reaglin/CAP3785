CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Introduction to Data Analytics and the Analytics Lifecycle

This module establishes the conceptual foundations of data analytics, exploring how organizations extract actionable insights from data. Topics include the four types of analytics — descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive — as well as the phases of the CRISP-DM lifecycle framework.
