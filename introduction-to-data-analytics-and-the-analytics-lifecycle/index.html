CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Introduction to Data Analytics and the Analytics Lifecycle

This module introduces the field of data analytics — what it is, why it matters, and how organisations use it to make better decisions. Learners explore the four core analytics types (descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive) and the CRISP-DM lifecycle framework, grounded in real-world case studies and business applications.
