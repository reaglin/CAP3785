CAP3785 — Data Analytics and Visualization

Module 1 — Introduction to Data Analytics and the Analytics Lifecycle

This module establishes the foundations of data analytics: what it is, why it matters, and how structured frameworks guide real-world projects. Explore the four analytics types, the CRISP-DM lifecycle, and industry case studies that connect theory to practice. Use the resources below to navigate readings, presentations, assessments, and assignments for this module.
