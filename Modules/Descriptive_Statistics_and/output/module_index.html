Descriptive Statistics and Probability Distributions

This module introduces the foundational statistical concepts used in data analysis — from summarising datasets with measures of central tendency and spread, to understanding distribution shape and applying key probability distributions including the Normal, Binomial, and Poisson. Use the resources below to explore readings, review lecture slides, and test your understanding.
